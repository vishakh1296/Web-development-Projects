Transportation

Thunder Bay offers many services that can be helpful in getting you where you want to go. In all cases calling ahead and asking specific questions about what accessibility features are available is the best way to stay up to date, and to ensure that your needs are met.

Taxis

Two local taxi companies offer accessible vehicles.
Diamond-Lacey's - 807-622-2227
Roach's Taxi - 807-344-8481


Public transit

Transit Services provides public transit within the City of Thunder Bay city limits. Thunder Bay Transit conventional buses are 100% low-floor, accessible with ramps, a kneeling feature, on board stop announcements, and priority and courtesy seating. Trip planning features include pocket schedules, NextLift.ca, and GoogleMaps. Stop times are available by phone at 684-3744.

Lift+ specialized transit

Also operated by Thunder Bay Transit is the Lift+ System of specialized transit vehicles. Lift+ is a door-to-door specialized public transportation service for those who due to a disability are unable to use the accessible, conventional bus service for all or part of the trip
